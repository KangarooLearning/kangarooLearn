Welcome to Kangaroo Learning!

Kangaroo Learning is a small company focused on education, open source tech writing, and independent software research.

Kangaroo Learning primarily offers Introductory & Intermediate level Python Programming, and Introductory Network Engineering courses to individuals.

Tired of working through an online tutorial, or read books online? If yes come to Bratislava for an in-person learning experience that will expand your mind and skills. Taught in a round-table format with just 6-10 students, these courses take a deep dive into a topic with a mix of lecture, hands on exercises, projects, and group discussion. Plus, you'll experience the beauty of Bratislava's distinctive 'staremesto' or the english equivalent 'old town' , with a cup of coffee. You won't be disappointed.
